About Northland Schools Kano

Vision Statement

To be a leader in producing skilled technical professionals, empowering Nigeria to compete globally in technology, management, and entrepreneurship.

Mission

To provide quality education in technology and science, fostering entrepreneurial skills and character development. Mustibrah College equips students with the knowledge and expertise needed to excel in a dynamic digital world.

Philosophy

We aim to produce graduates with strong academic and practical skills for self-reliance and industry relevance. Our focus is on nurturing enterprising individuals ready to thrive during and after their studies.

History

Mustibrah Model School, established in 2009, is a renowned educational institution offering comprehensive learning across Nursery, Primary, Secondary, and Islamiyyah levels.

Mustibrah College Mustibrah College of Information Technology and Management Studies, building on the legacy of Mustibrah Model School, was established in 2021 as a polytechnic dedicated to advancing technical and management education in Nigeria.
